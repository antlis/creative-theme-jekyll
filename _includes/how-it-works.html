HOW IT WORKS

1. Selection Process

After you make a submission, we will get back to you within 24 hours to let you know whether your site is a good fit or not. We care about customer satisfaction and this requires that we only work on websites that we believe will allow us to make you a happy and referring customer.

2. Discovery

We gather all of the information that we need from you such as brand name, logos, colors, copy, type of website (multiple pages, parallax), overall message you want to convey, etc. To meet the deadline we rely heavily on the use of web themes and templates. We will send you a list of theme options to choose from.

3. Start

We agree on a start and end date for your project. Due to the volume of work that we receive, the start date may not be the same day that you make a submission on our website.

4. Progress

Our team will send you progress of our work to get your feedback around the 24 hour mark.

5. Delivery

We will deliver the final website files to you after 48 hours of the agreed start date.

6. Revisions

We will perform minor revisions within the following 24 hours free of charge.
